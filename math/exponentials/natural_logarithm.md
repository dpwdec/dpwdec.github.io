The **natural log** is simply tells you **what number to raise \\( e \\) to so that it equals another number**.

The **natural log** of \\( 2 \\) is \\( 0.6931 \\). So

\\[ e^{0.6931} = 2 \\]

## Notation

The **natural log is usually written as**

\\[ ln(x) \\]
